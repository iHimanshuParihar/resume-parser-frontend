Effortless Resume Parsing and Powerful Search: Unlocking Your Talent Treasure





Resume Analysis and Management

Welcome to our platform, where we simplify the process of managing and optimizing your resumes. Our service is designed to make the job application process more efficient and effective.


Upload Section

In the "Uploads" section, users have the convenience of uploading up to 10 resumes one by one. The process is straightforward and user-friendly. Our platform supports various document formats, but for optimal results, we recommend uploading resumes in PDF format.


Resume Analysis

Once a user uploads a resume in PDF format, our system automatically analyzes the data and identifies the most suitable designation based on the content. This automated analysis helps users categorize and organize their resumes efficiently.


Search Section

In the "Search" section, users can search for the best resume for a specific designation. The system allows users to filter results based on years of experience, enabling a more refined search. This feature is invaluable for employers and job seekers alike, providing a streamlined way to find the most relevant resumes.


Resume Management

Our platform also offers a comprehensive resume management system. In the "Uploads" section, users can view the resumes they've uploaded. Additionally, they have the flexibility to delete resumes directly from this section, providing full control over their uploaded content.


Key Features:

  • Effortless Uploads: Upload up to 10 resumes seamlessly, one by one.
  • Automated Analysis: Our system automatically analyzes PDF resumes to find suitable designations.
  • Advanced Search: Search for the best resumes based on designations and filter by years of experience.
  • Resume Management: View and delete uploaded resumes for enhanced control and organization.

Our platform is dedicated to making the resume management process efficient, allowing users to focus on what matters most: finding the right opportunities and candidates.
